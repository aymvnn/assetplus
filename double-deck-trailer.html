TECHNICAL FAQ

SPECIFICATIONS EXPLAINED

The Double Deck Trailer stands at 4.00m, well within the UAE maximum of 4.60m set by Cabinet Resolution No. 138 (2023). It clears all standard overpasses and loading docks without modifications. Width is 2.55m (max allowed: 2.60m).

The three variants differ in length and axle configuration. City (11.30m, 2-3 axles) is optimised for urban distribution. Standard (13.60m, 2-4 axles) covers most regional routes. Longhaul (16.15m, 3-4 axles) is designed for inter-emirate and GCC cross-border operations. All carry 53-55 pallets.

Loading time is comparable to a standard trailer. The lower deck loads via a standard dock, then the upper deck is raised hydraulically (takes approximately 2 minutes), and the upper level is loaded. Total additional time versus a standard trailer is under 15 minutes per full load.
