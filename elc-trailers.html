ELC FAQ

EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW

ELC stands for Extended Life Cycle. It is a Burgers Carrosserie factory program where double deck trailers that have completed their first operational cycle are returned to the factory, fully stripped down, rebuilt with OEM components, and re-certified to factory standards. The result is a trailer that performs identically to new — at a significantly lower investment.

In terms of performance, capacity, and compliance — they are identical. An ELC trailer carries the same 55 pallets, uses the same hydraulic systems, and meets the same UAE regulations. The difference is the investment level and delivery time: ELC trailers are available faster and at a smarter price point.

No. A generic used trailer has unknown maintenance history, no OEM certification, and uncertain remaining lifespan. An ELC trailer is factory renewed by the original manufacturer — Burgers Carrosserie — with genuine OEM parts, recalibrated systems, and full factory certification. It is closer to new than to used.

Yes. All ELC trailers are fully compliant with UAE Cabinet Resolution No. 138 (2023). At 4.00m height (max 4.60m), 2.55m width (max 2.60m), and within the 21m combined length limit, they operate legally on all UAE roads without special permits.

ELC trailers are available in the same configurations as new: dry/retail (plywood lining), frozen/chilled (dual-zone temperature control), and pharmaceutical (insulated, GxP compliant). Contact us to discuss which configuration matches your fleet requirements.
