FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DOUBLE DECK TRAILERS IN THE UAE

Yes. The Burgers Double Deck Trailer fully complies with UAE Cabinet Resolution No. 138 (2023). At 4.00m height (max allowed: 4.60m), 2.55m width (max: 2.60m), and within the 21m combined length limit, it operates legally on all UAE roads without special permits.

The Double Deck Trailer carries 53 to 55 EUR pallets per trip, depending on configuration (City, Standard, or Longhaul). A standard single-deck trailer carries approximately 33 pallets — making the Double Deck 60% more efficient per trip.

Fleet operators across Europe report a documented ROI of under 12 months. The savings come from fewer trips (less fuel, less driver cost, less toll), reduced CO₂ emissions (40% per pallet), and lower maintenance cycles. In the UAE and GCC, where route distances are typically longer, the ROI case is even stronger.
